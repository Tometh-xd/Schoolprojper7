About Us

This is a website for those who struggle with addiction to the internet, for those who have struggles with the cyberbullying. Our goal is to provide resources and support to help individuals regain control of their online habits and promote a healthier relationship with technology. Do not hesitate to reach out if you need help, we are here for you! You are not alone in this journey. So we welcome you to explore our website and take the first step towards a better balance between your online and offline life.
